# Gameplay Loop
1. Collect Income
2. Consume Food and Water
3. Generate Science and Industry
4. Resolve Research
5. Resolve Projects
6. Trigger Events
7. War Phase (if applicable)
8. Player Interaction Phase (RPG section)


### Trackable Items

Capability Badges - These badges make it easier to track achievements and gives leaders an idea of where they are in the world. This will also determine the age they're in.

Construction Center

### Research
These foundational technologies are available to all leaders at game start and require no research progress.  

Basic Logistics - Simple Vehicles, Trucks and roads  (Enables Hauling, Trading, and Transportation)  
Basic Agriculture - Enables Food Production  
Basic Science and Manufacturing Knowledge - Enables basic Construction and Energy production  
Basic Public Services - Enables Local Governments, Policing, Social Services, and Health services
