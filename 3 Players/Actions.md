## Player Possible Actions
Declare Research (Research can be Tech, Health, Industry, Science, Social) (Research stations and Science centers contribute to research. Multiple research can happen per science centers. Must have at least one Research) \
Declare Manufacturing Projects ( Each Industry Center can help manufacture items but the logistics need to support it.) \
Declare Construction Projects \
Manage Economy \
Inspire, Interact or direct Populance (RPG can lead to random events inspired by the player)

Establish Agencies

Post/Recruit Unique NPCs
