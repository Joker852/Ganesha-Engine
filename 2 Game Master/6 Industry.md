# Industry
Eureka D20
Each Industry Center adds 100 Industry per month
Research center focusing on industry provides 200 and you get to roll 1d6 every month (Spend 300 science and 100 industry to choose reward)
1 - gain 1 tech
2 - 5 nothing
6 - advance current project by 25 percent (if no project gain 200 industry)

Industry Centers need 100 populance to run at full capacity [50 to run at half capacity and can be 0 to not function]

Industry Center Maintenance is 20,000 per month
Waste produced depends in capacity and project but if running minimum waste is 100 waste
